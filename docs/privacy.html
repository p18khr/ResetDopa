Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

ResetDopa™ ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the ResetDopa™ mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email and Authentication Data — Your email address and password (securely handled by Firebase Authentication)
  • Health Data — Mood entries, urge logs, task completions, and daily activity tracking for dopamine management and habit building
  • Optional Data — Profile information, preferences, feedback

Health Data Automatically Collected

  • Step Count & Activity Recognition — Your daily step count via ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION and BODY_SENSORS permissions to track physical activity and encourage healthy movement habits
  • Movement & Motion Data — Device accelerometer and motion sensors to detect activity patterns
  • Health-Related Usage — When and how you use the app's wellness features (mood logging, urge tracking, breathing exercises, stretching guides)

Other Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information — Device type, operating system, app version
  • Analytics Data — Screens visited, features used, session duration
  • Crash Reports — Error logs and stack traces (currently disabled)

Local Storage

We use AsyncStorage (on-device storage) to remember your login session, store app preferences, cache daily content, and track local progress between syncs. No cookies or third-party tracking is implemented.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your account and provide app services
  • Track your progress and generate statistics
  • Send daily reminders and notifications (with your permission)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Share your data with marketers or brokers
  • Store credit card or payment information
  • Track you across other apps or websites

2.1 Health Data Usage & Purpose

What health data we collect: Mood entries, urge logs, mood-triggered task recommendations, step/activity counts, and usage of wellness features (breathing exercises, stretching guides, meditation timers).

Why we collect it: Health data is essential to ResetDopa's core functionality — providing personalized dopamine reset support, tracking recovery progress, and recommending beneficial activities based on your mood and activity level.

How we use it:

  • Personalize your daily recommendations based on mood and activity
  • Track your streaks, adherence, and progress over time
  • Suggest healthy movement (stretching) when you're sedentary
  • Generate wellness insights and statistics
  • Improve the app's ability to support your recovery journey

Who has access: Only you and your account have access to your health data. We never share or sell your health information to third parties, marketers, or research organizations without your explicit written consent.

3. Permissions & Sensor Access

ResetDopa requests the following permissions to provide health and wellness features:

  • ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION — Access to step count and daily activity data. Used to track physical movement and encourage healthy habits as part of your dopamine reset journey.
  • BODY_SENSORS — Access to accelerometer and motion sensors. Used to detect physical activity patterns and suggest movement-based exercises (stretching) when recommended.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM — Ability to set daily reminders for mood logging and task completion.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Permission to send notifications for reminders, task suggestions, and achievement badges.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Permission to restart reminders after device reboot to maintain consistent habit tracking.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device's Settings > Apps > ResetDopa > Permissions. Revoking permissions will limit app functionality but will not delete your data.

4. Data Storage & Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Authentication — Firebase Authentication (Google/Firebase managed)
  • App Data — Google Cloud Firestore (encrypted at rest and in transit)
  • Local Storage — Your device's AsyncStorage (encrypted by OS)

Security Measures

  • End-to-end HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore encryption at rest
  • Industry-standard security practices and firewalls
  • No sensitive data (passwords, credit cards) stored in the App

Your password is encrypted by Firebase Authentication and never visible to ResetDopa servers.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active, including task completions, streak history, mood and urge logs, and badge data.

You may request deletion of your account at any time through the Settings screen. All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Deletion is irreversible.

If we receive a legal request to preserve data, we may retain information longer than our normal retention period to comply with legal obligations.

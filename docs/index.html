The 30-day reset is a structured framework built into ResetDopa. Each day, you log your current mood (high dopamine, baseline, or low dopamine), and the app recommends tasks tailored to your state. As you complete these tasks and maintain your streaks, you're rewiring your brain's response patterns. The 30-day period is based on neuroplasticity research—the time typically needed to form lasting habit changes. You can repeat the reset cycle as many times as needed.

Micro-Quests are small, actionable tasks designed for your current mood state. Instead of a generic "exercise" or "meditate" suggestion, ResetDopa generates specific, time-boxed activities: "Do 5 minutes of diaphragmatic breathing," "Take a 10-minute walk," or "Write 3 things you're grateful for." Each quest is calibrated to help reset your dopamine baseline and build momentum. Completing quests earns you Calm Points and contributes to your streak.

ResetDopa requires an internet connection to sync your data across devices and access the AI companion.
